The Harland Site Hunt

Fifteen stops across the new site, plus a timed FAQ lightning round. The point isn't trivia, it's the philosophy and the words we're about to put in front of the world.

How to play
  • Each clue points to a fact on a real page. Find it, type it, and the next stop unlocks (with fireworks).
  • Ten core stops carry a letter and reveal why that line matters; collect all ten to crack the final cipher.
  • Five Explore the site stops tour the rest of the pages for points.
  • Clues are 3 points; a hint drops it to 1; you can always skip. Then take the FAQ Lightning Round against the clock for bonus points.

Who's playing?

Pick yourself. Ruby plays the Chinese site. Jasmine's hunt is the canonical brand facts. Everyone's on one leaderboard.
