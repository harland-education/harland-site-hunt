The Harland Site Hunt

Ten stops across the new site, plus a FAQ lightning round. The point isn't trivia, it's the philosophy and the words we're about to put in front of the world.

How to play
  • Each clue points to a fact on a real page. Find it, type it, and the next stop unlocks (with fireworks).
  • Every right answer reveals why that line matters for how we talk about Harland, and earns a letter.
  • Collect all ten letters to crack the final cipher.
  • Clues are 3 points; a hint drops it to 1. Stuck? You can skip (you'll still see the answer). And don't miss the FAQ Lightning Round for bonus points.

Who's playing?

Pick yourself. Ruby plays the Chinese site. Jasmine's hunt is the canonical brand facts. Everyone's on one leaderboard.
